How it works?

Generate Weekly Ulam

With just one click of the Generate Now button, Filipino Ulam (meals) from our database will appear. They were gathered and carefully selected ideally for your Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner. Each time you generate, you can either remove meal/s from the list shown or directly add the suggested Ulam (meals) to your account.

Automatic Meal Plan

The Ulam(meals) you've added to your account can be created as your meal plan for the week. No more worries with your meal plan we got ya!

Add Meal Plan to Calendar

Once you're done with your meal plan for the week, you can then add it to your phone Calendar for easy access and reminder.

Collect Recipes from Websites

You can also search for recipes anywhere and just simply copy the link and paste it to your Ulam (meal).

Why Plan?

 

One of the key benefits of Ulam (Meal) Planning is that it helps reduce the frustration when planning about what meal should you cook for next week. Sometimes we can't figure out the type of meal we'd love to prepare because of certain factors like the health conditions of one of the family members or having a picky-eater child. Therefore, we aim to provide parents, especially Moms, Inays & Mamas, who are always being asked one question: Ma, Anong Ulam? So we, at Weekly Ulam Planner, prepared hundreds of Filipino meals in our database that will give you the best combination of healthy meals for your breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Lessen Time Planning

With just one click, you'll have Ulam (meal plan) ready for breakfast, lunch and dinner! You just need to search for recipes anywhere online and copy the link then add it to the Ulam (meal) generated for your reference.

Budget Ulam

Once you know the meals (Generated Ulam), you can carefully select the ingredients and calculate the cost of every meal. You can also avoid dining out and buying takeaways.

Lessen Stress & Anxiety

If you are always being asked by your husband/wife & kids on what's going to be your Ulam (meal)?... you can easily answer them by just clicking the Generate Now button or by simply looking into your Weekly Ulam Planner calendar. No more fuss and worries.

Lessen Food Waste

Meal planning has been proven to help reduce food wastes. By organizing your food groceries and knowing the exact amount of ingredients that you need for the week, you'll be able to cook the ingredients that you only need and save the remaining for your next meal prep.

About Us

We are a Filipino family based in The Netherlands. Together with my husband, we developed this website available for every Filipino who loves to cook & eat Filipino Ulam (Meals). I've tried a lot of meal planning methods available online, however, I couldn't find any website that generates and suggests Filipino Ulam Meals automatically for breakfast, lunch & dinner and can attach Ulam recipes at the same time. Since we both love coding, we came up with this idea and built this website for every Filipino user. We aim to lessen the frustration & stress when creating their Weekly Ulam Planning. We also want to promote our traditional healthy Filipino Ulam meals in every family wherever they are in the world.
